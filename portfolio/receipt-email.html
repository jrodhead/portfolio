Problem

When viewing an email receipt on small screens or windows (like on a mobile phone), the layout and line wrapping makes the entire receipt difficult to read. Two issues were brought to our attention:

  1. When viewing an email receipt on small screens or windows (like on a mobile phone), the layout and line wrapping makes the entire receipt difficult to read
  2. Blank space displays on printed receipts when data is configured to display but does not exist on a transaction
(screenshot)

Objective

Improve readability of sales receipts sent by an in-store Point of Sale system. The email must be generic enough to be applied across 400+ stores across the country with much of the content being variable options within the software or from the transaction.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Receipt must be easy to read and comprehend on the top 5 most-used email clients according to https://emailclientmarketshare.com/oReadability.Receipt
  • Average time for a first-time customer to correctly identify an individual piece of information on a receipt (for example, the price of an item): Target: 5 seconds; Stretch: 3 seconds; Fail: 10 seconds;
  • If data is configured to print but does not exist on the transaction, the placeholder for that data should be removed
