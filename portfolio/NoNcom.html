Design

I am the resident web and graphic designer for my band North of Neptune, which is a hobby project between a couple friends and I. The design is an extension of the album artwork that I created for our EP and, as a nod to the more tangible album experience prior to the internet, intentionally leans a little closer to art than usability. The result is an interesting and slightly mysterious way to navigate a website while you listen to some jams.

Toolbox

HTML5, CSS3, Responsive Web Design, JavaScript (jQuery)
